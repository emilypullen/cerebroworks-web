Privacy Policy — UpTrack

Effective February 8, 2026 · CerebroWorks

App Store

This Privacy Policy describes how CerebroWorks handles information in connection with the UpTrack iOS application.

What We Collect

  • Email address, if you contact us or join our mailing list
  • Anonymous, aggregated app analytics
  • Crash reports via Apple's crash reporting

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your win logs, notes, or any content you enter in the app
  • Location data
  • Personal data sold to any third party
  • Data used for advertising

How We Use Information

  • To respond to support requests
  • To send launch notifications (only with your consent)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage

All personal data (wins, notes, logs) is stored locally on your device via Apple's secure storage. CerebroWorks has zero access to this data.

Third-Party Services

  • MailerLite — email list management (only if you sign up)
  • Apple App Store — download and purchase processing
  • Apple Analytics — optional, aggregated usage only

Your Rights

Delete the app to remove all locally stored data. To delete any email data we hold, contact hello@cerebroworks.com.
