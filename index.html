Privacy Policy — UpTrack

Effective February 8, 2026  ·  CerebroWorks

This Privacy Policy describes how CerebroWorks ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the UpTrack iOS application.

What We Collect

  • Email address, if you contact us or join our mailing list
  • Anonymous, aggregated app analytics
  • Crash reports via Apple's built-in crash reporting

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your win logs, notes, or any content you enter in the app
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not sell personal data to any third party
  • We do not use data for advertising

How We Use Information

  • To respond to support and contact requests
  • To send launch and update notifications (only with your consent)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage

All personal data (wins, notes, logs) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage. CerebroWorks has no access to this data.

Third-Party Services

  • MailerLite — email list management (only if you sign up)
  • Apple App Store — download and purchase processing
  • Apple Analytics — optional, aggregated app usage

Your Rights

You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data. To request deletion of any email data we hold, contact hello@creceralabs.com.
