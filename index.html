This Privacy Policy explains how CerebroWorks ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the UpTrack iOS application ("the App"). By using UpTrack you agree to the practices described here.

No data is used to track you across other apps or websites. UpTrack does not use advertising networks, data brokers, or App Tracking Transparency (ATT).

Data We Collect

  • Contact Info (Email): Collected only if you email us directly. Used solely to respond to your message. Never shared with third parties for marketing.
  • Diagnostics (Crash Logs): Anonymous crash reports submitted automatically via Apple's crash reporting system. Contains no personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Data (Aggregate Analytics): Anonymous, aggregated feature-usage counts (e.g., "button tapped 200 times"). Cannot be linked to any individual user.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your win logs, counters, notes, or any content you create in the App
  • Location data (precise or coarse)
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Health or fitness information
  • Contacts, browsing history, or search history
  • Device identifiers for advertising

How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to support or contact requests (email only)
  • To diagnose crashes and maintain app stability (crash logs)
  • To understand aggregate feature usage and guide improvements (analytics)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or unaffiliated third parties.

Third-Party Services & SDKs

  • Apple App Store: Handles app distribution and purchase processing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Crash Reporting: Receives anonymous crash diagnostics. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Analytics (optional): Receives anonymous, aggregate usage data if you have opted in to share analytics with developers in iOS Settings. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Each third party listed above provides at least equivalent privacy protection as described in this policy. UpTrack contains no advertising SDKs, social login SDKs, or cross-app tracking libraries.

On-Device Data Storage

All content you create in UpTrack (win logs, counters, notes, categories) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure local storage. CerebroWorks has no technical ability to access, view, or retrieve this data. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all on-device data.

Data Retention

  • Email correspondence: Retained for as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry, then deleted within 12 months.
  • Crash logs: Retained by Apple per Apple's policies; we receive only anonymized summaries.
  • On-device data: Stored on your device until you delete the app.

No Tracking — App Tracking Transparency

UpTrack does not track users across other companies' apps or websites. We do not request the ATT (App Tracking Transparency) permission. We do not use fingerprinting, hashed identifiers, or any alternative tracking methods.

Your Rights

  • Access: Contact us to request a summary of any personal data (email) we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Email hello@cerebroworks.com to request deletion of any correspondence data we hold. Uninstall the app to delete all on-device data.
  • Correction: Contact us to correct inaccurate personal data we hold.
  • Objection / Restriction: Contact us to object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Opt-out of analytics: Disable sharing analytics with app developers in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.
  • California residents (CCPA): We do not sell or share personal information. You have the right to know what data we hold and to request its deletion.
  • EU/UK residents (GDPR): You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Security

On-device data is protected by Apple's iOS security model including device encryption and Secure Enclave. We use standard security practices for any email communications. No system is 100% secure; if you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

Children's Privacy

UpTrack is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly. Contact us at hello@cerebroworks.com if you believe this has occurred.

International Data Transfers

If you contact us by email, your email address may be stored on servers located in the United States. By contacting us, you consent to this transfer. We ensure any transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
